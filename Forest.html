The Forest

The forest of Augglandia offers many different things to do. There are many different species in the forest including: brown bears, black bears, foxes, wolves, and even a unicorn. There are many different campsites in the forest. If you want you can go hike and stay at the campsites. This is one of the most popular activites in the forest.
